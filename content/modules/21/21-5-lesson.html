03 · What Is a Soul-Bound Token (SBT)?

The Technology Behind Your Permanent Credential

A Soul-Bound Token (SBT) is a special type of NFT that cannot be transferred to another wallet once minted. It is permanently bound to the wallet address that receives it - your "soul." The concept was proposed in 2022 by Vitalik Buterin, Puja Ohlhaver, and E. Glen Weyl in their paper "Decentralized Society: Finding Web3's Soul."

An SBT is like a degree certificate that is stapled to your identity forever. You cannot sell your B.Tech degree to someone else - and you cannot transfer your MST Developer Certificate either.
🏅 Why MST Uses SBTs for Certificates

MST chose SBTs because a credential must be non-transferable to have value. If certificates were regular NFTs, people could buy and sell them, destroying trust. An SBT proves that you specifically - the wallet holder - completed the program. Employers can verify this on-chain without contacting any central authority.

05 · How to Claim Your Certificate

Step-by-Step Claim Process on MST Mainnet

1
Receive the Claim Email
After Demo Day, you'll receive an email from academy@mstblockchain.com with a unique, one-time claim link. This link is tied to your registered wallet address.
2
Connect BridgeKey Wallet
Open the claim page in a browser with BridgeKey installed. Ensure you're on MST Mainnet (Chain ID 4646). The page will prompt you to connect.
3
Sign the Claim Transaction
A small gas fee (approximately 0.0001 MST) is required to mint the SBT. Confirm the transaction in BridgeKey. The certificate is minted directly to your connected wallet.
4
Verify on MST Explorer
Immediately after minting, you'll receive a link to view your certificate on mstscan.com. The SBT will appear under your address's token holdings with a special "SBT" badge.
⚠️ Important Notes

• The claim link is one-time use only. If you lose it, contact support immediately.
• The SBT is minted to the wallet you connect - make sure it's the wallet you want associated with your professional identity.
• You need a small amount of MST for gas. If your balance is zero, use the MST Bridge or contact the academy for a gas stipend.
• Once minted, the SBT cannot be transferred to another wallet. Choose your wallet wisely.
